  • Log podaci — IP adrese, tip pretraživača i vreme pristupa za bezbednost i otklanjanje grešaka

This is a beta service intended for friends and family testing. We take your privacy seriously even during early development.

Account Information

  • Email address — For account creation, login, and essential communications
  • Name — To identify you on the platform and in booking communications
  • Phone number (optional) — For booking coordination between renters and owners
  • Profile photo (optional) — To help build trust between users

Usage Data

  • Booking history — Records of rentals you've made or received
  • Search queries — What you search for to improve vehicle matching
  • Messages — Communications between renters and vehicle owners
  • Reviews and ratings — Feedback you provide about rentals

Technical Data

  • Log data — IP addresses, browser type, and access times for security and debugging
  • Error reports — Technical information when something goes wrong (to fix bugs)
  • Device information — Basic device type for responsive design

Vehicle Owner Data

  • Vehicle details — Make, model, year, photos, location, and availability
  • Pricing information — Daily rates and rental terms you set
